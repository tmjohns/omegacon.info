About the Convention

OmegaCon Geelong is an all-day games convention with opportunites to play a range of board, card and role playing games.

OmegaCon is for everyone, if just discovering modern boardames or a seasoned veteran. Come along and play a game; you can learn something new or teach others your favourite game.

We will have a library of games available to play, and you are welcome to bring your own games to share with others.

We are community-run and we would welcome volunteers to help us with future events. If you're interested in volunteering, please contact us.

Tickets

Tickets are available for purchase through Humanitix.

Prices:

  • Adults: $10
  • Children (12-18): $5
  • Children (11 and under): Free

Tickets are available at the door, but we recommend purchasing in advance to guarantee your spot.

Community

Geelong has a thriving gaming community with a number of Facebook groups facilitating regular meetups:

  • Board Game Fun (Geelong) - meet fortnightly (Thursdays) at Belmont Library.
  • Surf Coast Board Game Society - weekly (Wednesdays) at Torquay Angling Club
  • Geelong Tabletop - weekly (Thursdays) at Guf Geelong
  • Geelong Gamers Guild - weekly (Mondays) role-playing games at the Geelong Club

In addition, we run a monthly meetup on the second Friday each month at Geelong West Neighborhood House. Contact us for more details.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of admission?

Tickets cost $10 for Adults, $5 for Children aged 12-18. Ages 11 and under are free.

Are there age restrictions?

The event is open to all ages, however all children must be supervised by their parent or guardian.

How can I participate in games?

Most of the day is open board gaming, where you can pick up a game, find players and go.
We have a schedule on Aftergame.app to organize to play particular games at specific times.

What games will be available?

We will have a range of board games, card games, and role-playing games available. You can also bring your own games to share with others.

Will there be food and drinks available?

Yes, there will be snacks and drinks available for purchase at the venue.
The venue is also located near to the Pakington St shopping district, which features multiple cafes and restaurants.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is parking available at the venue.
